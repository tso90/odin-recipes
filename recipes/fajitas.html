Fajitas

Description

This tasty mexican dish combines all the family favourites: chicken, vegetables and flavour, all in fun sized wraps!

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Red Bell Peppers
  • Red Onions
  • Flour Tortillas
  • Seasoning mix of your choice

Steps

  1. First chop up your vegetables and chicken
  2. Fry your vegetables and chicken in a pan with oil with your chosen spice mix
  3. Serve up on your flour tortillas and enjoy!
